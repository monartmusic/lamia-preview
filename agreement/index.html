What's Not Included

To keep expectations clear, the following are outside the scope of this agreement:

  • Music production, mixing, or mastering
  • Music video production or editing
  • Graphic design beyond ad creatives (album artwork, logos, press photos, etc.)
  • PR and press outreach
  • Spotify playlist pitching
  • Social media management (posting, scheduling, community management)
  • Website development or maintenance
